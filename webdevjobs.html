In addition to looking up jobs, I perused the Bureau of Labor Statistics for more information about web developer jobs. It appears from 2014-2024, it is expected that web developer jobs are to grow approximately twenty-seven percent, which is much faster than average. It makes sense because more businesses are embracing e-commerce and providing services online; therefore, a well-designed website or web application is necessary. Another statistic that stood out to me was the median pay. In 2015, the median pay was $64,970 per year, or roughly $31.23 per hour. I can imagine that growing within the next few years with the rising population of the Internet.
