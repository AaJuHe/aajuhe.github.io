Dark Web

By Aaron Hearne

Web Sites vs. Web Apps HTTPS UTF-8 Dark Web Web 2.0

Prior to the TED talk, I knew nothing about the Dark Web. I have heard of places and sites on the Internet where “immoral” and possibly illegal transactions such as pornography and drug trades occur without much scrutiny; however, I did not know what it was called at the time.

It does appear that the popularity of the Dark Web is gradually increasing, especially with those who value their privacy while surfing the web. I am a fairly private person myself, both in person and over the Internet, so I can relate to those who do not want to let others know what they are doing. Having said that, I cannot see myself, outside of possibly wanting to experience what it is like, using the Dark Web anytime in the near future. Furthermore, with it becoming more mainstream over time, it appears privacy on the Dark Web would no longer be such a strong point, especially with others using it for “legitimate” reasons. It will be interesting to see where everything goes from here.
