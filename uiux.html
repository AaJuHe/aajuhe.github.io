UI/UX Designer

By Aaron Hearne

Become a Web Developer Full Stack vs. Front End Web Development Jobs UI/UX Designer Web Technology Fundamentals

From what I understand, it appears that a UI/UX designer is focused on the design and interaction of a website whereas a web designer is primarily focused on the design. Other than that, there does not appear to be much of a difference between the two.

Speaking of differences, there is one between a UI designer and a UX designer. A UI (User Interface) designer is more focused on the layout of a website. It makes sense for a UI designer to focus on designing the site to be easy on the users’ eyes. On the other hand, a UX (User Experience) designer is focused on making sure that the site is easy to navigate and access. No matter how the site looks, if the user does not feel comfortable navigating the site, he/she will not have a pleasant time browsing it. Although the two have different roles, UI and UX designers work hand in hand in order the ensure users are able to have a great experience on their (UI and UX designers) sites, which is probably why the two are usually linked together.
