Upon looking through a number of editors, I decided to go with Sublime Text, at least for this class. It appears this is the editor of choice for many programmers and developers; therefore, this may be the best option for me at this time. In the future, I would most likely use Atom because it is the most intriguing to me, plus it is open source which I am very fond of.

As far as setting up the Code Along folder, the biggest challenge was trying to fully comprehend the instructions. Admittedly, I did get confused in some places and therefore, I had to watch the video(s) explaining the steps more times than I would have liked. However, I do think I was finally able to get the setup correct and hopefully there will not be as many problems as I would like while doing the Code Along assignments this semester.
